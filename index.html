GALATEK AI
GALATEK AI, is GALATEK’s AI-powered platform that redefines industrial asset management across the entire asset lifecycle. Built with unparalleled flexibility and depth, GALATEK AI supports both SaaS and on-premises deployments to meet the unique operational needs of the energy sector, seamlessly integrating with every stage—from design and construction to commissioning, operations, and maintenance.
GALATEK AI brings data and operations to life with immersive walkthrough simulations, live streaming, and real-time visualisations, making complex decision-making faster and more intuitive. The platform’s dynamic design reviews, advanced analytics, and construction sequencing streamline processes, while its Asset Information Management (AIM), remote monitoring, and autonomous operations empower operators with the precision and confidence needed for high-performance operations. GALATEK AI integrates effortlessly with third-party systems and amplifies its capabilities through GALATEK’s proprietary AI solutions, including the Virtual Flow Meter, Advanced Process Control, AI Autopilot, and a suite of digital twins.
